Brief History of Kiwanis

The Kiwanis Club was founded in Michigan in 1914 to be a vehicle for service in the community and beyond and to provide fellowship for those interested in providing service. It was especially interested in children, and one of its first international projects was to combat childhood thyroid insufficiency.
